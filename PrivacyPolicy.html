XrayBox Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

XrayBox is a local-first moving inventory app. It helps you record move names, destinations, rooms, box numbers, box contents, photos, QR labels, and label-printing settings.

Data Stored On Your Device

XrayBox stores the information you enter in the app on your device using Apple's local app storage. This may include move details, room names, box contents, QR payloads, and box photos.

Import And Export

If you export a move, XrayBox creates a file containing that move's details and photos. You choose where that file is saved and who receives it. Anyone with access to an exported file may be able to view its contents.

Camera And Photos

XrayBox asks for camera access to photograph box contents and scan QR labels. XrayBox may ask for photo library access if you choose an existing photo or if camera capture is unavailable.

Accounts And Sync

XrayBox does not require an account and does not sync your inventory through external servers.

Tracking And Analytics

XrayBox does not include advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Deleting Data

You can delete boxes and moves inside the app. You can also remove all local XrayBox data by deleting the app from your device. Exported files must be deleted separately from wherever you saved or sent them.
